5 CHARACTERS FROM NOLI ME TANGERE

Crisostomo Ibarra
-Son of Don Rafael Ibarra
-Studied in Europe for 7 years
Don Rafael Ibarra
-Father of Crisostomo Ibarra
-Friends with Kapitan Tiago and Lieutenant Guevarra
-Rich
Maria Clara
-Is not the son of Kapitan Tiago
-Was taken under Tiya Isabel after Maria Clara's mother died giving birth
-Arranged to be married to Ibarra
Captain Tiago
-Real name is Santiago de los Santos
-Moreno at pandak, with a round face
-Married to Pia Alba, who died after childbirth
Lieutenant Guevarra
-Highly respects and befriends both Don Rafael and Crisostomo Ibarra
-Made Father Damaso leave after exposing a dark secret of his
-Wishes that Crisostomo does not end up like his father
